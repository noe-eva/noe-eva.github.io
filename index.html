Noëmi Aepli

I am a computational linguist currently conducting postdoctoral research at the University of Pennsylvania.

My research is driven by a deep fascination with dialects & language varieties, and the unique challenges they present for natural language processing (NLP). I am particularly interested in how computational methods can be adapted to handle the richness and diversity of human language.

I earned my PhD in Computational Linguistics and Language Technology from the Department of Computational Linguistics at the University of Zurich, Switzerland.
